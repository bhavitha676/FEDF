What We Offer

Ten modules.
One seamless experience.

01
Itinerary Builder
Drag-and-drop day planner with smart scheduling, time conflict detection, and duration estimates for every activity.
02
Activity Search

03
Map View
Interactive map showing your day's activities, routes, and distances — all pinned geographically in sequence.

Hotel Activities
Seamlessly integrate your hotel's spa, dining, gym, and concierge-curated experiences directly into your day.

Transport Add
Book taxis, car rentals, trains, or private transfers and inject them directly between activities in your schedule.

Time Planner

PDF Export

Share Link

Hotel concierge dashboard to manage guest itineraries, approve activity bookings, add custom hotel offerings, and review analytics — all in a purpose-built admin interface.

The Process

perfect day.
